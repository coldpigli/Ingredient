Alerts

Alert boxes are used quite often to stand out the information that requires immediate attention of the end users such as warning, error, info or confirmation messages.

With Ingredients you can easily create elegant alert messages for various purposes by adding the contextual classes. You can also add an optional close/dismiss buttom to dismiss any alert.

Types of Alerts in Ingredients

We at Ingredients know that you love choices. Sweat not! We've got you covered. Alerts in Ingredients come in 4 main variants. They are:

  • Information
  • Success
  • Danger
  • Warning

Let's look at how these variations will look at your screen.

Simple Alert

A simple alert in Ingredients is of one of the following types. You can exclude putting in the icons if you want.

info
There are now over 200 people using Ingredients.
check_circle
We were successfully able to transfer the money
report
We were not able to transfer your money successfully.
warning
This action could potentially harm your computer
