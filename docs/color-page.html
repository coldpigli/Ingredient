Colors in Ingredient

Colors palletes are essential to any system. When used correctly, colour - along with good typography, can be one of the single most important aspects of website design - so getting the palette right at the start of the project is essential and should ideally be limited to two or three colours as to not complicate the website design and overwhelm users.
