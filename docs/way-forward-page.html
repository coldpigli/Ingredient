Way Forward

Ingredient is an open source styling framework, which helps Developers to use best styles and components, by using pre-defined classnames. Ingredient helps you to quickly build your Dream Project, by focusing much on Functionality, because we take care of your Designs

How can you help us?

Ingredients is open sourced. We would love for you to contribute to help make this better and and more robust. We would love it if you could help us to add on more components to this.

Also, Ingredients is overrideable. You can easily use Ingredients and then make your custom requirements match to your code. Just make sure you do not break it :D
